How to download RobotC:

1. Create an account on vexrobotics.com

2. Go onto your account and on the left-hand side, click on "software downloads".

3. Enter in your team number. (Your team must be registered! You can get the team number from your team coach.)

4. If you are playing Vex EDR, click on the link that says: ROBOTC for Vex Robotics 4.x (Windows EXE, for individual installation).

Disclaimer: This software only works on Windows machines. If you are running on Max or Linux software, look into using a different laptop or a virtual machine.
